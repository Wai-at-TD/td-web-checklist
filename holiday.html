Holiday page checks

Holiday pages are very similar to their respective hotel pages. The top section contains the same full width image, same navigation and rotating text. The image slider below the large image is also reused from the hotel page but the dimensions are smaller.

Please check the details of the holiday; the price, duration, date of departure and whether the details in the text content are correct.

Check the accommodation section for incorrect content about the room type, location and any other information about the hotel.
